Undergraduate Thesis

Thesis Topic: Finite Element Analysis of Progressive Failure in Composite Structures

  • Softwares used: ABAQUS, MATLAB
  • Element Analysis software ABAQUS was used to analyze Progressive Failure of Composite materials Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP) and Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP).
  • Total 14 ply stack orientations of the composites were simulated after structured meshing to predict the failure for each type under tensile loading. A notch was added in the center for localization of stress. The damages to the fiber and matrix progressed and resulted in failure of the material, observed from the contour plots.
  • MATLAB codes were used to obtain Stress vs Strain graphs and Force vs Displacement graphs.
